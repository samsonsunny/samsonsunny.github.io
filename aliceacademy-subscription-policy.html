By upgrading to Aliceacademy Premium, you agree to our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.

Subscription Period

Your billing cycle begins the day you sign up. If you chose the monthly billing period, your subscription will renew automatically each month, from the day you signed up. If you chose the annual billing period, your subscription will renew automatically each year, from the day you signed up.

When you upgrade to Aliceacademy Premium, all members of your account will have access to Aliceacademy Premium features.

The account admin who upgraded to Aliceacademy Premium will manage the subscription settings for the entire account using their Apple ID on their iOS device.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. When you cancel, your subscription will continue until the end of the current billing period, and your subscription will not automatically renew. You will continue to have access to Aliceacademy Premium features until your billing period elapses.

Features

Aliceacademy Premium includes all the features of the basic, free version of Aliceacademy, you can watch all video tutorials in premium also future premium features will be available to you

Managing Auto-Renewal

Your Aliceacademy Premium subscription will renew automatically at the start of your billing cycle. If your payment method expires or elapses, your access to Aliceacademy Premium features may be temporarily suspended until your payment method is updated.

You may view your subscription settings and billing history for Aliceacademy Premium using your iOS device's Settings menu:

  • 1. Open Settings and tap on your Apple ID name card at the top of the menu.
  • 2. Tap on iTunes & App Store, then tap your Apple ID at the top of the screen.
  • 3. Select "View Apple ID" and then tap Subscriptions.
  • 4. Select Aliceacademy: Video tutorials to access your subscription settings.
  • The subscription settings menu will allow you to cancel your Aliceacademy Premium subscription, or change between a monthly billing period. If you change your billing period, the change will take effect when the current billing period ends:
  • Aliceacademy does not maintain, store, or share payment information for subscribers who upgrade to Aliceacademy Premium using their Apple ID from an iOS device. Payments are processed by Apple and subscription information is tied to the user's Apple ID.
